Loss Prevention & Security
Shrinkage (theft, fraud, admin error) typically eats 1–3% of retail revenue. AI surveillance converts every prevented theft directly to bottom-line profit with no additional selling required — making it one of the highest-ROI AI investments in retail.
Direct Margin Protection
AI Video Surveillance & Behavior Analytics
🇺🇸 🇨🇳
Computer vision monitors CCTV feeds in real-time, detecting suspicious behaviors: concealing items, unusual lingering in aisles, organized theft patterns, and repeated unscanned items. Alerts staff immediately. Operates discreetly without invasive bag checks.
Example (US): Winters Market reduced theft substantially after AI monitoring. Laurel Ace Hardware reported a 50% drop in theft. High-risk sections see up to 60% shrink reduction within months of AI deployment.
Loss PreventionSupermarketMallElectronics
🇮🇩 High — CCTV infrastructure already exists; AI overlay is low cost
Savings: –30–60% shrinkage
AI Self-Checkout Fraud Detection
🇺🇸 🇦🇺
Computer vision AI monitors self-checkout for fraud: barcode switching, item concealment, "banana tricks" (scanning cheap item for expensive one), weight mismatches. Differentiates intentional fraud from honest mistakes. Halves error rate.
Example (France): A retailer using AI self-checkout vision reduced transaction errors from 3% to under 1.5%, targeting below 1%. The system correctly differentiates intentional fraud from honest errors (e.g., avocado misclassification).
Fraud PreventionSupermarketHypermarket
🇮🇩 Medium — Relevant as self-checkout adoption grows
Savings: –50% fraud rate at SCO lanes
AI POS Transaction Anomaly Detection
🇺🇸
AI monitors every POS transaction for anomalies: unauthorized cashier discounts, voided sales patterns, sweethearting (giving free items to friends), returns fraud. Flags for review in real-time. Addresses internal theft, often 30–40% of total shrinkage.
Example (US): 85% of payments professionals cite fraud detection as the best AI use case. PayPal's deep learning reduced fraud losses by 25%. Internal cashier theft via discount abuse is a well-documented challenge in Indonesian retail operations.
Internal TheftComplianceAll Formats
🇮🇩 High — Internal dishonesty is a known challenge; high ROI
Savings: –25–30% fraud losses
AI Payment Fraud Prevention
🇺🇸
ML models analyze payment transaction patterns in real-time, flagging stolen card usage, account takeovers, and false return/refund fraud. Adapts to new fraud patterns far faster than rules-based systems.
Example (US): 66% of merchants are currently using or planning generative AI for payment fraud management. Merchants report 5–25% increase in first-party misuse (friendly fraud) in 2024 — a growing threat with QRIS and e-wallet adoption.

Workforce & Staff Management
Labor is 25–35% of retail operating expenses. AI scheduling, task management, and performance analytics unlock significant cost savings while improving employee fairness and ensuring the right staff are on the floor when customers need them most.
Operational Efficiency
AI Workforce Scheduling & Labor Forecasting
🇺🇸 🇯🇵 🇰🇷
AI models predict staffing needs at 15-minute intervals using POS data, foot traffic, promotions, local events, and weather. Auto-generates optimal weekly rosters, flags coverage gaps, and manages shift swaps in real-time.
Examples: Walmart cut shift planning from 90 to 30 minutes with AI. MIT Sloan: 90% staffing prediction accuracy. McKinsey: 10–20% improvement in workforce utilization; 5–8% overtime reduction. Japanese retailers deploy this to manage structural labor shortages.
Cost SavingAll Formats
🇮🇩 High — Indomaret, Matahari, Transmart multi-location chains

AI Agentic Task Management for Associates
🇺🇸
AI "digital workers" track all store tasks (restocking, cleaning, display setup, compliance checks) and assign them to associates via mobile app, prioritized by urgency and impact. Verifies completion via photo. Eliminates wasted labor hours.
Example (US): Deloitte's digital worker model assigns tasks via store app, tracks compliance via photo verification, and offers chatbot guidance when associates have questions — reducing wasted hours and improving in-stock consistency.
